Benefit #1 - Visualize the structure. Offload your working memory.

  • Representation matters.
    Solving a problem simply means representing it so as to make the solution transparent.
    - Herb Simon
  • It's easier when the structure of your thoughts is right in front of you.
  • Tango presents this structure to you.

Benefit #2 - Back and forth is necessary.

  • A good quote:
    Both optimists and pessimists contribute to our society. The optimist invents the airplane and the pessimist the parachute.
    - Gil Stern
  • Daniel Dennet argues that individuals are biased and good at arguing for extreme positions. They're specialists in one-sided arguments. This doesn't work well in isolation, but it does work well in groups where there is a back and forth.
  • Tango helps you simulate this back and forth by using Sims.
